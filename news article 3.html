BOOKA BRASS FEATURED IN 6 PART SKY ARTS TELEVISION PROGRAMME
Published: June 23rd, 2019


James along with Booka Brass are featured in a 6 part series on Sky Arts named "Tony Visconti's Unsigned Hereos"

The show is based on a concert organised by David Bowie's producer Visconti in London's Union Chapel where Booka Brass perform along with the likes of Stewart Copeland of The Police, Bob Geldof and Imelda May. The concert features pieces from the artists Visconti worked with including Bowie, T.Rex, Thin Lizzy, Paul McCartney and Wings and Morrissey.
