BOOKA BRASS TO PERFORM AT CORK OPERA HOUSE ONCE AGAIN
Published: December 26th, 2020


Booka Brass will once again be the headline act at the Cork Opera House as part of the Guinness Cork Jazz Festival.

This will be the fifth time the New Orleans style brass band have achieved this and they promise to continue the trend of executing an exciting and entertaining show with a new set of pieces being added to the repetoire.

The show will take place on Saturday 23rd October @ 22:00 with a support act yet to be annoucned. Tickets can be bough through the Cork Jazz Festival website here
