TICKETS TO SEE BOOKA BRASS AT VICAR STREET NOW AVAILABLE
Published: December 20th, 2020


On Thursday June 10th Booka Brass will take to the stage of Dublin's Vicar Street. This will be the second time the new orleans style brass band will perform in the famous Dublin venue.

2016 brought the launch of the band’s debut Album Chilled Milk, followed by a sold out Irish tour to support the release. The band turned away over a hundred fans from their debut sold out Cork show and finished the tour with a sold out show in one of Dublin's most prestigious venues, Vicar Street.

‘Booka Brass Band have every right to be claimed as Ireland’s best live act right now’, - Golden Plec
